## Base
Base - Are the step, frame and lower part of an escalator.

base-by : Metal base with black-yellow step. Modern Escalator.
base-g  : Metal base with gray step.
base-by : Gray yellow step.
base-vn : Vintage Escalator with wooden step.

## SIde
Side - are the side or escalator handrail.
side-fm : Full-Metal
side-gl : Glass
side-vn : Wooden side, only has a black handrail.
